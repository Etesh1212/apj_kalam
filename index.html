A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

1931-2015

Misile Man Of India

Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam, often referred to as the "Missile Man of India," was a renowned scientist, visionary leader, and the 11th President of India. Born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, Kalam emerged from humble beginnings to become a pivotal figure in India's scientific and political landscape.Kalam's journey began with his early education in Rameswaram and later at the Madras Institute of Technology, where he specialized in aerospace engineering. He played a crucial role in the development of India's ballistic missile and nuclear weapons programs, earning him widespread recognition for his contributions to defense research.
